ENGLISH SKILLS PROGRAM

Transform your English language skills with Grow Learners intensive learning program. The trainer will take up classes in small batches so that it becomes easy for learners to learn and improve communication skills.

Week 1 GRAMMAR

The first week will be on grammar which includes nouns, adjectives, subject-verb agreement, verbs, articles, pronouns, basic tenses, prepositions, phrasal verbs, conjunctions, modals, adverbs, and conditionals.

Week 2 READING AND LISTENING STRATEGIES

The second week will be focused on skimming, scanning, main idea, supporting ideas, understanding infographics, listening strategies. As reading and listening are the foundation of learning the language.

Week 3 VOCABULARY

The third week will be an interesting one on teaching new words. Emotions, colloquialisms, interests, idioms, personality, social situations all these things will be taught and assessed accordingly.

Week 4 WRITING SKILLS

The fourth-week session includes topic tone and audience, sketching an argument, structuring an argument, editing, proofing. Comprehensions will be practiced to improve the writing habit.
